Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our eye care services, working hours, locations and booking. Your vision is our priority.

Still Have Questions?

Our friendly reception team and expert optometrists are happy to help with bookings, pricing and general eye care advice across all our branches.

Email Support Book Online Call 081 348 9403 / 072 136 2199

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive eye care: general eye exams, low vision and binocular vision assessments, pediatric optometry, traffic certificates, contact lenses, spectacles and frame repairs. We use the latest diagnostic technology and stock trendy fashion eyewear for every budget.

Where are you located and what are your hours?

We have three branches:
• Ga-Makanye (Mankweng Mall) — Shop No: 7B, CNR R71 Polokwane Tzaneen Highway & Mankweng Roads, 0727
• Turfloop Toronto — House No: 568, Unit B Toronto, Opposite Traffic Department, Mankweng, 0727
• Polokwane Central — Letago Medical Zone, Suite 5, 57 Compensate St, 0699
Hours: Mon–Fri 8:00–17:00, Sat 8:00–13:00 (Sun & public holidays closed).

How do I book an appointment and what should I bring?

You can book online via the “Appointment” button or call 081 348 9403 / 072 136 2199. Please bring your ID, any current spectacles or contact lenses, and previous prescriptions or referral letters. For traffic certificates, bring the required ID documents as stipulated.

Do you accept medical aid? How do claims work?

Yes — we work with most South African medical aids. Bring your medical aid card and valid ID. Where available, we can claim directly on your behalf for eye tests, lenses and frames according to your benefits. Any co-payments or items not covered will be discussed upfront and settled in-practice. If you prefer, we can also provide a detailed quote to submit yourself.

What documents do I need for a Traffic Certificate (eye test)?

For driver’s licence/traffic eye certificates, please bring:
• SA ID/Passport
• Current driver’s licence (if renewing) or learner’s
• Existing spectacles/contact lenses (if you use them)
• Proof of address (some licensing offices require it)
• ID photos (optional — some departments capture on site)
We’ll complete the vision assessment and provide the signed certificate required by the traffic/licensing department.

Do you have affordable vision plans and eyewear options?

Yes. We believe everyone should access quality vision care. We tailor affordable plans and will always source the best lens and spectacle frame options to suit your needs and budget—without compromising on quality or style.
