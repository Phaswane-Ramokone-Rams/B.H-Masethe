Comprehensive Eye Examination

Accurate prescriptions, comfortable vision and proactive eye health

Our comprehensive eye exam checks your vision, refines your prescription and screens for ocular health concerns using modern, non-invasive equipment.

Whether you’re updating your spectacles/contact lenses or addressing visual discomfort, our experienced team provides personalised recommendations that suit your lifestyle and budget.

Your Exam Includes:

  • Refraction & prescription updates
  • Ocular health screening
  • Digital vision testing & comfort assessment
  • Lens & frame recommendations
  • Follow-up guidance
