Our Story

Community-focused eye care — personalised exams, honest advice, and frames you’ll love.

Clear Vision. Caring People.

At MASETHE B.H OPTOMETRISTS, we believe great eye care starts with listening. From comprehensive eye exams and paediatric vision to contact lenses, repairs and frame styling — our team delivers friendly, professional care tailored to your lifestyle.

With convenient branches in Polokwane Central, Ga-Makanye (Mankweng Mall) and Turfloop Toronto, we’re proud to serve families, students and professionals across Limpopo with modern diagnostics and a warm, welcoming experience.
