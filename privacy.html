Privacy

Your vision is our priority — and so is your privacy. This page explains how MASETHE B.H OPTOMETRISTS handles your personal information.

Effective Date: February 27, 2025

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information when you visit our practices in Polokwane Central, Ga-Makanye (Mankweng Mall) and Turfloop Toronto or use our services, including online bookings and communications.

1. Introduction

When you use our optometry services, you trust us with your information. We respect that trust and work to protect your information while providing professional eye care and personalised service.

This policy helps you understand what we collect, why we collect it, and how to update or request deletion of your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver our services, such as scheduling appointments, performing eye examinations, dispensing eyewear and lenses, and issuing traffic certificates.

2.1 Information You Provide

Examples include:

  • Name, contact details and communication preferences
  • Medical and vision history relevant to your care
  • Prescription details for spectacles and contact lenses
  • Payment details where necessary

2.2 Automatic Information

When you use our website or booking tools, we may collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • Usage logs and approximate location (when enabled)
  • Cookies or similar technologies to improve experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide eye care (e.g., exams, prescriptions, low/bino vision care)
  • Process orders for spectacles, contact lenses and repairs
  • Arrange appointments and send confirmations or reminders
  • Issue traffic certificates where requested
  • Maintain security, comply with legal requirements, and improve our services

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

4.1 With Your Consent

For example, when coordinating with lens laboratories or referring you to another healthcare provider.

4.2 For Legal or Safety Reasons

Where required to comply with law, protect our rights or the safety of patients and staff, or prevent fraud and abuse.

5. Data Security

We implement safeguards to protect information, including:

  • Secure storage of patient records
  • Restricted staff access on a need-to-know basis
  • Encryption and secure transmission where appropriate

6. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable laws, you may:

  • Access or update your personal information
  • Request deletion where appropriate
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll update the effective date above. Your continued use of our services signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
